The Great Migration

Tracking wildebeest across the Serengeti Masai Mara, and how predators respond.

Background

In the heart of East Africa stretches an endless plain — the boundless lands of the Serengeti and the Maasai Mara. The terrain rolls gently beneath the burning sun, where the tropical winds mark the rhythm of the seasons — rains and droughts that breathe life into the grasslands. When the rains come, thunder rumbles across the sky and tender shoots rise overnight; when the dry winds return, dust drifts over cracked earth and the rivers shrink to silver threads.
